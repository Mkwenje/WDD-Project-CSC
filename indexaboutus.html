About Us

Community Social Center was founded in 2021 by three students named Tadiwa Mkweje, Dylan Earls and Paul Webb.

The three founders have been researching homelessness in Ireland for many years. With this research they have identified many issues as to why there are so many homeless people in Ireland and what we can do about it. In 2021 when we set up the company we started out with only one small soup kitchen ran by volunteers. We noticed that many people would turn up and we would often run out of food. Since we had so many people turn up we decided to expand and create more soup kitchens. This has gotten a lot of attractions and many people have been donating money, clothes and food that we can supply the homeless with. We have since come a long way from just a small soup kicthen. We now have over 15 soup kitchens across Ireland. We now also have facilities that give away the free clothes donated to us as well as shelters that house over 400 homeless people across Ireland.

Soup Kitchens

We have 15 soup kitchens located across Ireland. This feeds hundreds of people per day. All the staff in these kitchen are volunteers.

We get many great people every day brining in fresh ingredients for us to use for our meals. We offer a variety of different meals and they are all completly free.

We open our soup kitchens from around 8am to 6pm everyday ensuring that everyone has time to get a meal.

Shelters

We have three huge shelters in Ireland. Two are located in Dublin and one in Cork.

These shelters house hundreds of people from all ages for up to 3 months. We supply clean blankets,a matress and a warm place to stay. We also have warm water for people to shower.

These facilities are open 24/7 and anyone without a home can apply at the door to be housed inside of them.

Free Clothes

We have many different locations across Ireland where we set up racks and give out free clothes. We typically offer tea and snacks at these locations as well as haircuts.

We have given away thousands of clothes to the homeless across Ireland and we strongly urge people to give away any unneeded clothes to our facilities.

We try and open everyday from 10am to 4pm in our locations depending on how much supply we have. It is a first come first serve basis where nobody is discrminated against.
